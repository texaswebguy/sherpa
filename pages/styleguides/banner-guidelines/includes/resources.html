Banner Template Files

  • These template files must be used to create all banner assets for Dell.com
  • Do not create one-off banner sizes (dimensions) or placements that have not been established in the Dell Design Library.
  • Do not alter the template files in any way that has not been outlined in these standards.
  • Do not break one banner up into multiple smaller banners on the page.
  • Do not stack banners on top of each other or next to each other, unless specified by documented exceptions or approved page templates. This rule has been in place since the launch of Next Gen and is reflected within the page templates approved for the site.
  • Each banner within a Rotating banner, Godzillatron or Pokemontron must have a call to action and must link out.
  • Moodboards do not have call to actions and do not link out.
  • Moodboards cannot be placed in a rotating banner, godzillatron or pokemontron.
